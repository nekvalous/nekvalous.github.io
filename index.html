About

I am working at the German Cancer Research Center (DKFZ) and the National Center for Tumor Diseases (NCT) Heidelberg. I am located at the Center for Quantitative Analysis of Molecular and Cellular Biosystems (Bioquant).

I am founding editor and executive editor of the open access peer-reviewed journal ImmunoInformatics (Elsevier).

You can check my profile on: LinkedIn, Google Scholar, ResearchGate, ORCID, Scopus, and Web of Science.

I am a hobbyist photographer.
