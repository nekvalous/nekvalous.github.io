My name is Dr. Nek Valous.
I am a research associate based in Heidelberg.

Currently, I work at the German Cancer Research Center (DKFZ) and the National Center for Tumor Diseases (NCT) Heidelberg in the field of computational biomedicine and interdisciplinary physics.

The development of computational methodologies to analyze and connect medical data to actionable clinical information has become the critical bottleneck in precision medicine. Robust multiscale and knowledge fusion approaches for analyzing medical data with accuracy and reproducibility are required. This paradigm is revolutionizing the scale and potential applications of translational studies, thus creating a need to develop mathematical and computational methodologies to meet emerging data analysis challenges.
